Overview

Purpose

Intro should say: Hello Friends! Welcome to Eastern Idaho's White Water Rafting Guides and Tours! Join us for a big splash of fun and adventure! We take all levels from beginner to experienced. Book with us today! The purpose of this website is to invite people to book a tour to go white water rafting and give info about what they need to do and what it is.

Audience

The audience here is anyone from beginner to experienced and anyone interested in WWR. The target is probably 20yrs and up because they would be the ones paying for the tours/guides. But it should be accessable and understandable to all ages of users.
